About Us Your Donation is Always Powerful Our Approach

Change 4 Change is a community powered organization without any government support. We Thank you individuals and corporations like you for your support to feed the needy, educate the public about hunger issues and address the root causes of hunger here in YYC.

Every single ingredient we provide our clients was sourced through the contributions of community members. From the can to the truckload – we move food to people where they need it – when they need it.

Your support is critical in our fight to end hunger. For every $1 you donate you help us secure 3 meals for our neighbors. There are a number of ways you can help.

  • Online Donation form (see below)

  • Online with our Virtual Food Drive Program

  • Become a monthly donor

  • By calling 403-444-5555

Our Mission

Healthy Food

People living in poverty often have limited access to healthy foods that can help reduce the risk of conditions such as heart disease, stroke, high cholesterol and diabetes. So we encourage and educate our donors on choosing nutritious food, particularly those that are considered heart healthy. These items are desperately needed to help all charities and food banks in Calgary.

Changing Minds

While perishable (fresh and frozen) foods cannot be collected for food drives, there are plenty of healthy options readily available in the canned and packaged food aisles of your local grocery. Here are some suggestions.

  • Fruits and Vegitables

Changing Communities

Our leadership partner Canadian Atlantic is committed to heart health for all Canadians. Over the holiday season, the CA Holiday Train brings music and good cheer to towns across the country while encouraging people to donate healthy food to food banks in Calgary. This past December, the CA Holiday Train supported food banks across Calgary by raising $1 million and 45,000 kg of food! Thank you, Canadian Atlantic!
